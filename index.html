How are you going to market Chimes Consulting?

by: Darwin A. Medel

Marketing involves finding your target market and identifying issues and pain points that they may be looking to resolve, and then reaching out to them through various means to let them know about your solution and then hopefully get them to become your customers.

As a provider of I.T. solutions, Chimes Consulting may best be marketed with the following:

1. Use of New Technology

Being up to date and able to utilize new tools and technology allows for the maximization of efficiency and quality.

2. Showcasing of Previous Works and Testimonies from Satisfied Customers

Having other people vouch for the quality of a service will attract other clients.

3. Use of Social Media and Online Blogging Platforms

Using platforms that are highly popular will surely provide a wider reach of audience.

4. Smart and Competitive Pricing

Affordable and appropriately priced products and services attract the attention and consideration of customers.
